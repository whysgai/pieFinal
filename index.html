Simple Berry Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 c berries, washed and dried (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 c white sugar
  • 1/4 c "instant" tapioca
  • 1 tbs lemon juice
  • 1 tbs butter, in small pieces

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Prepare crust and place in bottom of pan.
  3. Sprinkle berries with sugar, tapioca, and lemon juice.
  4. Toss until evenly combined.
  5. Pour berry mixture into pie.
  6. Dot with butter pieces and cover with upper crust.
  7. Seal edges and cut slits in top to vent.
  8. Bake 45 to 50 minutes.

Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 c pecan halves
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs butter (melted)
  • 1/2 c dark corn syrup
  • 1/2 c heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 c brandy or whiskey
  • 1/2 tbs vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Prepare crust and place in bottom of pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine eggs, sugar, salt, butter, syrup & cream.
  4. Stir in vanilla, brandy & pecans.
  5. Pour mixture into pie.
  6. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes.

Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 12 key limes (or 4 regular limes)
  • 14 oz sweetend condensed milk
  • 3 egg yolks (large or extra-large)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 530°.
  2. Zest and juice the limes, storing separately.
  3. Beat eggs and zest with electric mixer until pale and thick (~ 5 minutes).
  4. Add condensed milk, beat until thick again (~ 3 minutes).
  5. Whisk lime juice into mixture.
  6. Pour into pre-baked graham cracker crust.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes, and let cool completely.

Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8 firm cooking apples
  • 1/2 to 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbs butter, in small pieces

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. Prepare crust and place in bottom of pan.
  3. Pare, core, and slice the apples.
  4. Sprinkle apples with sugar, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
  5. Toss vigorously.
  6. Fill pie shell evenly, piling slightly higher in the center.
  7. Dot with butter and top with upper crust.
  8. Seal edges and cut slits in top to vent.
  9. Bake for about 50 minutes.
